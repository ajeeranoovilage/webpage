About Us

Kerala Literature Festival 2025

KLF, the largest literary festival in Asia, is a free event that draws hundreds of thousands of people from diverse backgrounds each year. For many, especially the youth and the general public, it has become an eagerly anticipated annual gathering—a celebration of art, culture, music, and literature. Kerala Literature Festival offers a unique experience for all, making it a true celebration for the public. Register as an attendee to stay informed about upcoming events and special offerings at the venue. Take the opportunity to meet your favorite speakers, celebrities, Nobel Prize winners, Booker Prize recipients, Oscar winners, and Olympic medalists. Don't forget to sign up as an attendee. See you in January on the beach with your friends, for a spectacular event - KLF.
